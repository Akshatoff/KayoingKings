About Us

EvalEnergy was born from a shared passion for chess. Frustrated by the complexities of opening theory, our founder sought a way to play chess based on pure positional understanding. This vision ignited a spark, and together, we embarked on a journey to transform this idea into a reality. EvalEnergy is the result of countless hours of development, dedicated to empowering chess players to focus on strategic depth rather than memorized openings."
